Mark Cuban

Mark Cuban (born July 31, 1958)[2] is an American billionaire entrepreneur, television personality, and media proprietor whose net worth is an estimated US$4.8 billion, according to Forbes, and ranked No. 177 on the 2020 Forbes 400 list.[3] He is the owner of the Dallas Mavericks professional basketball team of the National Basketball Association (NBA) - with which he won the 2011 NBA Championship as owner - and the co-owner of 2929 Entertainment. He is also one of the main "shark" investors on the ABC reality television series Shark Tank.
